Discography

A Look Into Taylor Swift's Albums

Since 2006, Taylor Alison Swift has released 12 studio albums, 4 rerecorded albums, 32 compilations, 5 extended plays (EPs), and 4 live albums. She is remarkable for being able to cross over from country, to pop, to alternative, with each album (or "era") containing its own aesthetic.

Release and Information Songs Awards/Recognition/Records Latest Video Fun Facts!

The Life of a Showgirl is the name of Taylor Swift's 12th studio album, released on October 3, 2025. It marked her return to producers Max Martin and Shellback, who produced Red (2012), 1989 (2014), and reputation (2017). It was announced on the podcast New Heights , hosted by her fiance and his brother, Travis and Jason Kelce, on August 13, 2025. It consists of 12 songs, with 1 feature. The album is primarily pop, with elements of rock, and heavily features burlesque, cabaret aesthetics.
